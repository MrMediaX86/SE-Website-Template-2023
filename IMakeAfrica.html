I MAKE AFRICA | HARARE

Exhibition Date December 2nd, 2017
Role Art, Virtual Reality
Project Exhibition
Visit Full Exhibition Here

I Make Africa | Harare is a web documentary & virtual reality art exhibition documenting the everyday people creating Africaâ€™s next great cities. With the climate of political change, the first installment of the project was filmed in the city of Harare (Zimababwe), where we interviewed & photographed 6 rousing individuals who chose to share stories about their lives, experiences, hopes and works in the city.

Through storytelling by fusing art and virtual reality technology, we help give a unique insight into the real lives of the people on the streets of Harare, away from the stereotypical images and perceptions usually driven by the media.
