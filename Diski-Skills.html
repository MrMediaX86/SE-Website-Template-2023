Diski Skills

Project Date October 27th, 2018
Role Augmented Reality, Gaming
Client Internal
Visit Full Documentation Here

A patented 2-player soccer strategy card game created in collaboration with Kimard Studio. The educational game was designed as a STEAM innitiative project based on the rules of soccer that see's players challenging each other, with the ultimate goal of collecting all cards to win. The game was developed as a tool to help train cognitive skills in kids through gaming.

The game was developed and created whilst on residency at the Goethe Institute & the SA Innovation Hub. Thanks to Steven Tu for invaluable contribution on game design.
