Anergi Group

Project Date September 12th, 2018
Role Identity, Branding
Client Archway Cooper
Visit Full Rebrand here

We were tasked to reimagine the Anergi brand logo to signify strong governance frameworks and development of a singular African identity, that clearly distinguishes itself as a leading power platform on the continent. The African continent needed to be a central component of the logo in reimagining the current logo.

Our Solution: As energy is cyclical, we infused the element of the circle shape into the visual logo design so that the circular shape will be represented in all the branding touchpoints
